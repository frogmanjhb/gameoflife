# Police Lieutenant - Job Role Document

## Authority Model

- **May issue fines independently**
- **Evidence must be logged**
- **Fines are reviewed by Lawyer and Teacher**
- **Police do not decide final outcomes alone**

## Core Purpose

The Police Lieutenant ensures that rules are respected and that consequences are applied fairly and consistently, with a strong focus on support before punishment.

## Tangible Responsibilities

### Daily (As Needed – Not Constant)

- Observe rule compliance during the school day
- Respond when issues are reported
- Issue fines only when rules are clearly broken
- Record:
  - who
  - what happened
  - which rule was broken
  - evidence (short written note)

### Weekly (10–15 minutes)

- Submit fine log to:
  - Financial Manager (for payment)
  - Lawyer (for review)
- Review repeat issues
- Meet briefly with HR Director to discuss patterns
- Support Mayor during:
  - town meetings
  - elections
  - emergencies

## Enforcement Limits

- **Cannot:**
  - increase fines
  - remove privileges
  - cancel fines
- **Can:**
  - refer repeat issues to HR or Mayor
  - recommend further action

## Tone & Approach

- **Supportive first**
- **Explain the rule before enforcing it**
- **Focus on restoring order, not punishment**
- **Consistency matters more than severity**

## Town Hub Permissions

- Log incidents and fines
- View rules and penalties
- See fine history (read-only)
- Submit notes for Lawyer review

## Physical Board Role

- Build and manage police station
- Visible presence during:
  - disputes
  - elections
  - emergencies
- **No authority over builds or land**

## Job Details

- **Salary**: $7,000 per period
- **Company**: Town Police
- **Location**: Police Station
- **Category**: Governance & Administration
