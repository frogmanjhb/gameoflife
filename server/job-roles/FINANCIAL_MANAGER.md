# Financial Manager - Job Role Document

## Authority Model

- **May move money independently**
- **All actions must be logged and visible**
- **Operates with oversight, not restriction**
- **Teacher retains ultimate authority**

## Core Purpose

The Financial Manager runs the daily financial engine of the town. This role ensures salaries, fines, bonuses and taxes are processed accurately and on time.

## Tangible Responsibilities

### Daily (Minimal – only when needed)

- Respond to questions about:
  - balances
  - transactions
  - payments
- Record:
  - fines from Police
  - bonuses from teachers
- Check for obvious transaction errors

### Weekly (Primary Workload – Payday & Tax Day)

- Pay all salaries
- Collect taxes
- Process:
  - fines
  - bonuses
  - approved adjustments
- Transfer funds to the Town Bank
- Prepare a clear financial summary for:
  - Chartered Accountant
  - Mayor (high-level only)

## Error Handling

- **Cannot fix mistakes independently**
- **Must:**
  - flag errors
  - document what went wrong
  - submit to Accountant for review
- **Corrections only happen after approval**

## Relationship to Accountant

- Operates as an equal operational partner
- **Financial Manager = execution**
- **Accountant = oversight and audit**
- Weekly handover of data is required

## Student Access

- Students may ask:
  - questions about how systems work
  - clarification on their own finances
- **Cannot disclose other students' balances**

## Town Hub Permissions

- Process deposits and deductions
- View all accounts
- Generate transaction summaries
- Flag anomalies for review

## Physical Board Role

- **No building authority**
- Visible role during:
  - paydays
  - tax collection
  - major town events

## Job Details

- **Salary**: $7,800 per period
- **Company**: Town Finance
- **Location**: Finance Office
- **Category**: Finance
