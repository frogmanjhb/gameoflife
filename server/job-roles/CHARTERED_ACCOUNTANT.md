# Chartered Accountant - Job Role Document

## Authority Model

- **Advisory only**
- **Cannot move money**
- **Cannot block transactions**
- **Power comes from analysis, evidence and credibility**
- **Teacher remains ultimate authority**

## Core Purpose

The Chartered Accountant safeguards the financial integrity of the town. This role exists to ensure accuracy, transparency and long-term sustainability of the town's economy.

## Tangible Responsibilities

### Daily (Active, Short Checks)

- Scan financial records for:
  - missing entries
  - unusual transactions
  - inconsistencies
- Answer high-level questions about:
  - budgets
  - financial health
  - sustainability
- Flag concerns early (no fixes yet)

### Weekly (Structured Audit)

- Audit:
  - salaries
  - taxes
  - fines
  - bonuses
- Review Financial Manager reports
- Identify financial risks or trends
- Prepare a financial health summary for:
  - Mayor
  - Teacher

## Relationship to Financial Manager

- Works as an equal professional partner
- **Financial Manager executes**
- **Accountant reviews and analyses**
- Disagreements are escalated with evidence, not authority

## Intervention Limits

- **Cannot force spending limits**
- **Cannot cancel transactions**
- **Can:**
  - warn
  - highlight risks
  - recommend caution
- **Final decisions sit with Mayor and Teacher**

## Reporting Line

- Reports to both Mayor and Teacher
- **Mayor receives summaries**
- **Teacher receives full detail**

## Town Hub Permissions

- Read-only access to all accounts
- View full transaction histories
- Submit audit notes and warnings
- Flag issues for teacher review

## Physical Board Role

- **No building authority**
- Visible during:
  - budget discussions
  - economic crises
  - end-of-term reviews

## Job Details

- **Salary**: $8,500 per period
- **Company**: Town Finance
- **Location**: Accounting Office
- **Category**: Finance
