# Lawyer - Job Role Document

## Authority Model

- **The Lawyer advises only**
- **Cannot block or enforce decisions**
- **Influence comes from quality of reasoning and evidence**
- **Final decisions sit with Mayor / Police / Teacher**

## Core Purpose

The Lawyer ensures the town operates fairly, consistently and within the constitution. The role exists to slow down bad decisions and protect due process.

## Tangible Responsibilities

### Daily (Ongoing, Light)

- Answer questions about:
  - rules
  - fines
  - rights and responsibilities
- Clarify what the constitution allows
- Support students preparing disputes or appeals

### Weekly (Structured)

- Review:
  - fines issued by Police
  - disputes escalated by HR
- Prepare written legal advice for:
  - Mayor
  - Council
- Participate in dispute resolution sessions
- Check that actions taken align with the constitution

## Dispute Handling

- **Decisions are based on written evidence only**
- **No emotional arguments**
- **Reviews:**
  - incident description
  - rules involved
  - recommended outcome
- **Produces a legal opinion, not a verdict**

## Relationship with Police

- Reviews fines and enforcement actions
- **Flags:**
  - inconsistent penalties
  - rule breaches
- **Cannot cancel fines directly**
- Reports concerns to Mayor and Teacher

## Constitution Control

- Interprets constitution only
- **Cannot rewrite or amend laws**
- May highlight unclear or conflicting rules
- Can recommend clarification (not changes)

## Town Hub Permissions

- View constitution and all laws
- View fines and disputes
- Submit legal opinions
- Flag constitutional concerns

## Physical Board Role

- **No building authority**
- Visible role during:
  - hearings
  - council discussions
  - elections disputes

## Job Details

- **Salary**: $8,000 per period
- **Company**: Town Government
- **Location**: Legal Office
- **Category**: Governance & Administration
