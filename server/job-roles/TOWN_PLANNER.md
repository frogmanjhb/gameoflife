# Town Planner - Job Role Document

## Authority Model

- **Town Planners approve land use and building independently**
- **Once a Mayor is elected, Planner + Mayor work together on major decisions**
- **Planner decisions stand unless overturned by the Mayor (teacher veto still applies)**

## Core Purpose

The Town Planner is responsible for the design, growth and organisation of the town. This role ensures the town is logical, sustainable and fair across all biomes.

## Tangible Responsibilities

### Daily (5–10 minutes)

- Answer questions about:
  - where buildings may go
  - zoning rules
  - biome requirements
- Check physical builds for compliance
- Update land ownership and zoning records on the Town Hub

### Weekly (15–20 minutes)

- Release available land plots
- Run the land tender process
- Approve or reject building applications
- Update:
  - town map (physical board)
  - land registry (digital)
- Meet briefly with:
  - Civil Engineer (roads)
  - Architect (builds)
  - Mayor (planning alignment)

## Sustainability & Rules

- **Enforces biome-specific sustainability rules**
- **Checks:**
  - correct materials
  - required energy systems
  - environmental adaptations
- **Does not award bonuses** (that stays with Finance / Mayor)

## Conflict & Enforcement

- **Cannot issue fines directly**
- **Can:**
  - freeze a build
  - flag illegal construction
  - report issues to Police + Mayor
- **Illegal builds cannot continue until resolved**

## Team Structure

- **Two Town Planners**
- **Split responsibilities by biome or zone**
- **Must agree on major planning decisions**
- **Encourages collaboration and consistency**

## Town Hub Permissions

- Edit land registry
- Approve / reject land tenders
- Flag compliance issues
- View town-wide development stats

## Physical Board Role

Controls placement of:
- houses
- public buildings

Co-signs infrastructure placement with Civil Engineer

Final check before builds are considered "official"

## Job Details

- **Salary**: $7,500 per period
- **Company**: Town Government
- **Location**: Planning Office
- **Category**: Governance & Administration
- **Note**: There are TWO Town Planner positions (x2)
